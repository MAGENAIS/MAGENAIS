# 🗺 ROADMAP

> *Building the world's most extensible GENAI Operating System.*

---

# Vision

MAGENAIS is evolving from a browser-based AI application into a fully modular, extensible, GENAI Operating System.

Our long-term mission is to create an open platform where developers, researchers, creators, educators, and organizations can seamlessly integrate AI models, workflows, tools, and agents without vendor lock-in.

This roadmap outlines the planned evolution of the project over multiple architectural phases.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every milestone follows these core principles:

- 🌐 Browser-First

- 🧩 Modular Architecture
- ⚡ Performance by Default
- 🔒 Secure by Design
- 🤝 Community Driven
