# Vision

MAGENAIS's long-term vision is to become an open, modular, browser-first
GENAI Operating System — a single extensible platform where any AI provider,
workflow, or plugin can be composed together, rather than another single-purpose
AI chatbot or wrapper.

For the complete vision document, see [`docs/VISION.md`](docs/VISION.md) and
the architectural philosophy in [`docs/architecture/01-vision.md`](docs/architecture/01-vision.md).
