# Frequently Asked Questions

A short FAQ. For the complete version, see [`docs/FAQ.md`](docs/FAQ.md).

**What is MAGENAIS?**
An open, browser-first GENAI Operating System that unifies dozens of AI
providers and modalities (text, image, video, audio, speech, music, documents,
research, games) behind one consistent interface.

**Do I need a backend server?**
No — the app runs entirely in your browser. You bring your own API keys for
whichever providers you want to use.

**Is it free?**
MAGENAIS itself is open-source and free. Individual AI providers you connect
may have their own pricing/free tiers.

**How do I add a new provider?**
See the [Provider SDK](docs/providers/PROVIDER_SDK.md).

**How do I write a plugin?**
See the [Plugin SDK](docs/plugins/PLUGIN_SDK.md).
